Introduction

I'm Paula. I'm 21 and in love with everything that beeps, buzzes and connects to the Internet. If R2D2 had wifi support I'd probably be married to him.

The whole story:

Technological devices have always amazed me and the internet is my true home.
Obviously I would have played with Barbies if only they had carried a laptop instead of a purse.
Growing up in the wonderful town of Dresden and going to this amazing school I was often frustrated because everybody was so much smarter then me and seemed to understand everything related to natural sciences and IT better than me. I decided that I'd rather pursue studies in humanities. After 4 semesters at Zeppelin University where I study Sociology, Politics and Economics I noticed how much I actually miss the clarity and logic that accompany science. That's why I started an online-course for coding and quickly noticed that I was not alone on this. With an awesome team we brought CodeCats to life. An initiative that promotes code literacy, especially amongst women. This summer we organized a summercamp with about 50 participants who all learn to code. We're in this together and we're rocking it.
